Certificate and project
1. Sound Amplifier Circuit:
Designed a low-power audio amplifier using a BC547 transistor, resistors, capacitors, and a speaker. The circuit amplifies weak audio signals from a mobile phone to drive a small speaker. This project helped me understand transistor operation, biasing, and signal amplification.

2. Home Décor Lighting System:
Developed an LED-based lighting system for decorative indoor use. The setup used LEDs and switches to create aesthetic light patterns while maintaining energy efficiency. This project enhanced my knowledge of LED circuits and basic electronic design
